Sex Chicken

Description

Sex Chicken is a easy and delicious chicken dish using only 3 or 4 ingredients.

It is great on Burgers, Rice dishes or simply by itself.

Prep: 3 min - Cooking time: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • 1 packet of Mckormicks Chipotle and Roasted Garlic dry rub
  • Oil Spray

Steps

  1. Place chicken thighs in a mixing bowl
  2. sprinkle dry rub until chicken thighs are fully coated and mix around
  3. Place chicken in air fryer for 20 minutes at 200 degrees celsius (392 degrees fahrenheit)

Let sit for 2 min and then serve. Enjoy!
