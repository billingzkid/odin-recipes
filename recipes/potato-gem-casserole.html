Potato Gem Casserole

Description

This Tater Tot casserole is a quick and easy dinner that everyone will love.

Just four basic ingredients come together for this comforting dish.

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Total Time: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef Mince
  • 1 Can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Potato Gems
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef, then stir in the soup and seasonings.
  2. Transfer the beef to a baking dish. Top with Potato Gems, then the cheese.
  3. Bake until Potato Gems are golden brown

Let Sit for 2 minutes, serve and Enjoy!
